About Ghielicious Food

Ghielicious Food is a small local bakeshop in Lucena City, and is owned by Geraldine. We are specialize in cakes, cupcakes, cookies, breads, and custom made cakes and cupcakes. Ghielicious Food is located 5 kilometers from downtown lucena city and easily accessible to all residents of the community.

Food prides itself with the exceptional customer service and attention to detail to all the pastries we serve. The Ghielicious Food is not well recognized yet, but everyone who orders here comes back for much more. The pricing is relatively reasonable, but the food is surely amazing for being able to make delicious custom made-to-order cakes and pastries for all types of events and gatherings. Their strong reputation and work ethics has primed Ghielicious Food for success.
